Hi, I’m a PhD student at Department of Complexity Science and Engineering, The University of Tokyo (UTokyo), advised by Prof. Naoto Yokoya (in Machine Learning and Statistical Data Analysis Lab). I am also affiliated with RIKEN AIP as a junior research assistant. Prior to that, I worked at Tencent (腾讯) as a graphics researcher.
